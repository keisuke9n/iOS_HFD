Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2025

Introduction

Metamorph ("we," "us," or "our") operates the HF Diary mobile application ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Health Data

  • Blood pressure readings (systolic and diastolic)
  • Pulse rate measurements
  • Weight data
  • Symptom information (exercise, breathlessness, swelling, fatigue, appetite loss, insomnia)
  • Timestamps associated with health records

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage analytics (crash reports, performance data)
  • Language and region settings

HealthKit Integration

With your explicit permission, HF Diary integrates with Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read health data from other compatible apps
  • Write your recorded health data to the Health app
  • Synchronize health information across your devices

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Store and display your health records within the app
  • Generate statistics and trend analysis
  • Export your data in CSV or text format (Premium feature)
  • Provide personalized health insights

Data Processing

  • All health data is processed locally on your device
  • No health information is transmitted to external servers
  • Data export files are created locally and shared through iOS sharing mechanism

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All health records are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption protocols
  • No cloud storage of personal health information

Data Retention

  • Health data remains on your device until manually deleted
  • App deletion removes all stored health information
  • Export files are temporary and deleted after sharing

Third-Party Services

Apple HealthKit

  • Integration requires explicit user consent
  • Governed by Apple's HealthKit privacy policies
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings

Subscription Management

  • Subscription processing handled by Apple App Store
  • No payment information stored within the app
  • Subscription status verified through Apple's servers

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Full control over your health data input and deletion
  • Ability to export your data at any time (Premium feature)
  • Option to disable HealthKit integration
  • Right to delete all app data

App Permissions

  • Location: Not requested or used
  • Camera: Not requested or used
  • Microphone: Not requested or used
  • Health: Optional, for HealthKit integration only

Children's Privacy

HF Diary is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

This app is available globally. Health data processing complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • Local privacy regulations where applicable

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal health information to third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.
