1. About the Application

HF Diary (the "App") is a health management application designed for recording and managing cardiovascular health data. By using this App, you agree to the following terms of use.

2. Service Features

The App provides the following features:

  • Recording blood pressure (systolic and diastolic)
  • Recording pulse rate
  • Recording weight
  • Recording symptoms (exercise, breathlessness, swelling, fatigue, appetite loss, insomnia)
  • Data synchronization with HealthKit
  • Data visualization and statistical displays
  • Premium feature: Data export in CSV and text formats
