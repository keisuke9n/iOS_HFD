HF Diary Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Important: HF Diary prioritizes your privacy. All health data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

HF Diary collects and stores the following health information locally on your device:

  • Blood Pressure Data: Systolic and diastolic readings
  • Pulse Rate: Heart rate measurements
  • Weight: Body weight measurements
  • Symptoms: Information about exercise, breathlessness, swelling, fatigue, appetite loss, and insomnia
  • Timestamps: Date and time of each health record entry

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your health records within the app
  • Creating charts and statistical summaries
  • Generating symptom heatmaps and trends
  • Exporting data in CSV or text format (Premium feature)
  • Synchronizing with iOS HealthKit (when you grant permission)

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement the following data protection measures:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms
  • No Cloud Storage: Your health data is never uploaded to external servers or cloud services
  • Device Security: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • HealthKit Integration: When enabled, data is securely stored in iOS HealthKit with Apple's security standards

4. HealthKit Integration

HF Diary integrates with Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission:

  • We request permission to write health data to HealthKit
  • You control which data types to share with HealthKit
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • HealthKit data is governed by Apple's privacy policies

5. Data Sharing

HF Diary does not share your personal health data with third parties:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No data is shared with advertising networks
  • No data is sold to third parties
  • Export functionality allows you to share data at your discretion

6. Data Retention

Your data retention is under your control:

  • Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • You can delete individual records or all data within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • HealthKit data follows Apple's retention policies

7. Premium Subscription Privacy

For our premium subscription service:

  • Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect payment information
  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework
  • No personal or financial data is transmitted to our servers

8. Children's Privacy

HF Diary is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. International Users

HF Diary is available internationally. Regardless of your location, your health data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.
